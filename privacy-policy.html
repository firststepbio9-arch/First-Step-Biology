At First Step Biology, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our students, educators, and visitors. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our educational services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better learning experience, including

  • Name, Phone number, and Email address
  • IP addresses and device information
  • Service Address and related network information
  • User uploaded photos and IDs
  • Academic Data: Quiz results and course progress
  • Demographic data such as postcode and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

Data is used strictly for educational and administrative purposes

  • Enrolling users and tracking course progress
  • Sending administrative updates or opt-in newsletters
  • Improving curriculum through aggregated, anonymous data
  • Ensuring the security of the digital learning environment

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data We only share it with:

  • Service Providers: Secured platforms like AWS or Stripe for hosting and payments
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or for student safety

4. Your Rights and Choices

Under 2026 data privacy laws, you have the right to

  • Access & Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • The "Right to be Forgotten": Request account and data deletion
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from non-essential emails

5. Cookies and Tracking:

We use "Essential Cookies" to keep you logged in and "Analytics Cookies" to see which biology modules are most popular. You can manage your preferences through our Cookie Consent Banner at the bottom of the homepage.

6. Security Measures:

We implement industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for all data transfers. Sensitive academic records are stored in encrypted databases with restricted access to authorized faculty only.
