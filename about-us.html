Our Story

Every great journey begins with a single step—and for us, that step was taken at a simple whiteboard in 2011. First Step Biology wasn't built in a corporate office; it was built in the classroom.

Our founder, Snehashis Gorain, began his journey over a decade ago as a dedicated tuition teacher. Armed with only a marker and a passion for life sciences, he created a learning platform to provide the clarity every student needs.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between "I don't get it" and "Aha!" moments. Our goal is to transform complex theories into clear, actionable knowledge, ensuring that every learner has the confidence to ace their exams and build a successful career.

Our Vision

To become the leading Digital Sanctuary for sciences in India, making quality education accessible to every dreamer. We envision a future where 'First Step Biology' is the first name students think of when they want to transform their curiosity into a successful career in science.
